# Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data using MySQL and Power BI to identify sales trends, regional performance, category-wise sales, and business insights.

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Dataset containing sales, profit, customer, product, and regional information.

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: $2.30M
- Total Profit: $286.40K
- Total Orders: 5,009
- Profit Margin
- Average Order Value

## Dashboard Features

### Executive Dashboard
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders, Profit Margin, Average Order Value)
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Interactive Slicers (Year, Region, Category, Segment)

### Product Analysis Dashboard
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top 10 Products by Profit
- Product Details Table
- Cross-filtering between visuals
- Navigation Buttons for easy report navigation

## Key Business Insights
- The Technology category generated the highest overall sales and profit.
- The West region contributed the highest revenue among all regions.
- A small number of products accounted for a significant share of total sales.
- High sales do not always correspond to high profit, highlighting the importance of profitability analysis.
- Interactive filters enable dynamic analysis across regions, categories, customer segments, and years.
